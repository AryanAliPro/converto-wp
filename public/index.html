Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about converting AI-built sites to WordPress.

How do I convert a Lovable site to WordPress?

Export your Lovable project as a ZIP file from the Lovable dashboard. Upload it to ConvertoWP, select "Lovable" as the source platform, and click "Generate WordPress Theme." The tool will build your project, render every route with Puppeteer, and package a pixel-perfect WordPress theme with content editing built in. The entire process takes 2-5 minutes.

Can I convert a Bolt.new website to WordPress?

Yes. ConvertoWP fully supports Bolt.new projects. Export your Bolt project as a ZIP, upload it to ConvertoWP, select "Bolt" as the platform, and the converter will generate a fully functional WordPress theme that preserves your exact design, layout, and interactivity.

Does ConvertoWP support Base44 to WordPress conversion?

Absolutely. Base44 is one of the three platforms supported by ConvertoWP. Upload your Base44 project ZIP, select "Base44" as the source, and you will receive a WordPress theme that matches your original site 1:1 with full content editing capabilities.

Is ConvertoWP really free?

Yes, ConvertoWP is completely free with no hidden costs, no signup required, and no usage limits. You upload your ZIP, get your WordPress theme, and you are done. No watermarks, no trial periods, no feature gating.

Will my converted WordPress theme look exactly like my original site?

Yes. ConvertoWP performs a 1:1 pixel-perfect conversion. It builds your project locally, renders every route with a real browser engine (Puppeteer), and preserves all CSS, layouts, animations, and interactive elements. The WordPress theme will look and behave identically to your original AI-built site.

Can I edit the content after converting to WordPress?

Yes. Every converted theme includes a built-in WordPress content editor accessible from Appearance > Theme Content in your WordPress admin. You can edit headings, paragraphs, buttons, images, and more without touching any code. Changes appear instantly on the frontend.

Do forms work in the converted WordPress theme?

Yes. ConvertoWP automatically wires all forms in your converted theme to a WordPress REST API endpoint. Form submissions are stored as WordPress posts under Form Entries and trigger email notifications to the site admin. No additional plugins required.

How does ConvertoWP compare to WPConvert.ai and other paid converters?

ConvertoWP is a free alternative that specifically supports Lovable, Bolt.new, and Base44 in a single tool. Unlike paid SaaS converters, ConvertoWP requires no account, has no usage limits, and includes WordPress content editing and form handling out of the box. Paid tools like WPConvert.ai charge per conversion and may not support all three platforms.

What do I need to install the converted theme?

You need a WordPress site (self-hosted WordPress.org). Go to your WordPress admin dashboard, navigate to Appearance > Themes > Add New > Upload Theme, select the ZIP file you downloaded from ConvertoWP, and click "Install Now." Then activate the theme and your site is live.

Does it work with multi-page sites?

Yes. ConvertoWP automatically detects all routes defined in your React Router configuration and generates a separate WordPress template for each page. Whether your site has 1 page or 20 pages, every route is captured and converted.
